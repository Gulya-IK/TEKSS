About TEKSS

TEKSS is a renewable energy developer working in the UK energy sector.

We are a one-stop shop offering a full wrap-around development service and our projects benefit from our in-house development, planning, finance, legal, grid, communications and consultation expertise as well as excellent track records in these fields.

Our team has significant development and grid connection experience in the UK energy sector and a successful track record.

We are technology-neutral and seek to only develop the best possible projects for our clients and the communities that host the project sites.

We work to best practices and look to develop only the most environmentally-appropriate sites.
