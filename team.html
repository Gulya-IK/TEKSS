Our Team

Tom Brinicombe
Director, Land & Planning

In his two decades of working in renewables, Tom has worked on a range of energy technologies and types of projects and has held various roles in the fields of project management, development and communications.

Tom has an engineering background and worked at AMEC and Partnerships for Renewables and in 2010 he set up Gunsko, a multidisciplinary energy consultancy. He has worked extensively on wind energy schemes (both onshore and offshore), solar PV schemes and HVDC interconnector infrastructure.

Ethan Dempsey
Director, Finance & Legal

Ethan has worked extensively in the UK solar sector and has also developed large-scale solar schemes in Europe, the United States and the Middle East. In 2014 he founded SST, a developer of utility-scale solar power plants.

Prior to working in renewable energy development, Ethan worked in fund management, equity analysis and corporate finance. He is also a qualified solicitor.

Kevin Bergin
Director, Electrical Design & Connection

Kevin has had an extensive career in the electricity distribution industry. Having joined Manweb in 1989 he then moved across to Scottish Power Contracting in 1997.

Kevin has held numerous engineering positions at Alstom, Areva and latterly was Private Networks Manager at Bethall Power. In 2007 Kevin formed Electricity Solutions, an Independent Connections Provider.

Gulya Isyanova
Head of Development

Gulya has successfully project-managed the development of and the communications for numerous UK renewable energy projects. Her development experience is complemented by her previous work in energy policy and energy communications, leading to a deep understanding of the technical, environmental, political and community issues associated with energy development.

She has worked at Gunsko, Partnerships for Renewables and the Stockholm Network.
